How it works

01

Register for an account

Please start by creating an account. It will allow us to keep track of your interests.

02

GIve us more information

Once you signup, you will be asked a short survey of 10 questions. A small personality test. 

03

Tell us where you want to go

After we know your interests, you will tell us where you will be travelling to.

04

We will plan the best route

Once we know where you are traveling to, with the help of the Google API, we will plan the best possible route to hit those locations in the most efficient way.

05

Recomendations

Based on your answers from step 2, we will recommend stops along your route which will match your interests.

06

Incorporate

Lastly, as a user you can decide to include that location as a part of your travel plan
